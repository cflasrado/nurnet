College of Nursing IT Office

The College of Nursing IT Office works along with FAU's Office of Information Technology (OIT) to provide the necessary computing services to faculty, staff and students in multiple facilites both on and off campus. For assistance on IT matters a ticket must be submitted to jsingh@health.fau.edu. For tickets please note the following:

  • The subject on the email should clearly describe the problem
    Submitting a ticket with subjects like “help”, “problem”, “urgent” or if the subject is completely unrelated to the problem delays us in responding to the tickets as we have to read through the message to understand the problem. A small effort on your part will save us a bit of time and allow us to respond quicker.
  • Always reply to the ticket
    When following up on an issue with us, please reply to the ticket in your email inbox (rather than sending a new email). This will guarantee that the reply gets appended to the ticket on our end. This way, we have a trail/history of the problem and solution. Also, replying to a “resolved” ticket will re-open the ticket and notify us that you still have questions on that issue.
  • Avoid direct emails
    Please send the emails to jsingh@health.fau.edu rather than to any of us directly. Often, the person you direct it to is already working on some other issues (or perhaps out of the office) and this prevents the others (who are available) to be able to handle your issue right away.
  • Avoid Hallway Requests
    Please try to avoid making a request “in the hallway” as we are passing by you (as 99% of the time we are working on another ticket). Submit the ticket formally through the established process so that it is not lost in the queue.
